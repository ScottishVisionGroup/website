About the meeting:

This is a residential conference that starts late afternoon on friday and finishes around 1pm on Sunday. The full schedule will be published once abstracts have been accepted. The Muthu Newton sits on 21 acres of garden and woodland, bordering the town of Nairn, with sandy beaches and stunning views over the Moray Firth.

Getting here:

  • Airport - The Inverness airport is 9 miles away and can be reached by taxi, bus or train.
  • Train - Nairn station is about a 15-20 minute walk from the hotel.
  • Bus - the regional bus station is a 10-15 minute walk.
  • Driving - The driveway into the Muthu Newton in Nairn is just off the A96 in Nairn, about 15 miles from Inverness.
